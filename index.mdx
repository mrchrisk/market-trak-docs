# Welcome to Market Trak

Market Trak is an all-in-one property marketing platform built for estate agents. Generate professional market reports, automate social media, manage leads, and let AI handle the heavy lifting.

## What you can do

- **Generate property reports** with real market data, share them publicly, and track engagement

- **Create social media posts** with AI-generated images and schedule them to Facebook

- **Manage leads** captured from reports and valuations, then nurture them with email campaigns

- **Automate property feed posts** from your CRM listings directly to social media

- **Chat with an AI assistant** that knows your workspace, writes content, drafts emails, and queries your CRM

## Getting help

If you need assistance at any time, click your profile icon in the sidebar and select **Get help** to open a live chat with our support team.

You can also ask the [AI Assistant](/docs/user-guide/ai-assistant/overview) questions about your account, reports, or how to use any feature.
