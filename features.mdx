## Overview

Market Trak equips UK estate agents with powerful tools to generate hyper-local market reports, customise content for your postcode areas, and capture leads automatically. You create seller's guides, data sheets, and article reports using live UK property data.&#x20;

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Start with your local postcode to generate reports that position you as the market expert.
</Callout>

## Generating Seller's Guides

Seller's guides provide sellers with tailored advice based on live data.

### From the Dashboard

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Postcode" icon="map-pin" title-type="p">
        Input a UK (outer) postcode like `SW1A, CM21, N12`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select Format" icon="file" title-type="p">
        Choose from Seller's Guide, Data Sheet and Article Report templates.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Generate Report" icon="lucide-cog" title-type="p">
        Generate your reports, edit and choose your publishing options

## Customizing with Local Postcodes

Tailor every report to your patch.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Validate Postcode" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Enter valid UK format like `M1 1AA` or `SW1A 1AA`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview Data" icon="eye" title-type="p">
    See live stats before generating.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Brand It" icon="palette" title-type="p">
    Upload logo and set colors (default: #3B82F6).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<ParamField path="postcode" param-type="string" required="true" deprecated="false">
  UK postcode in standard format.
</ParamField>

## Automating Lead Capture

Embed opt-in forms in reports to capture seller inquiries automatically.

<Expandable title="Embed Code Example" default-open="true">
  Copy this snippet into your website or email.

  <CodeGroup show-lines="true" tabs={[]}>
    ```html
    <iframe src="https://dashboard.example.com/embed/form?reportId=123" width="100%" height="400"></iframe>
    ```

    ```jsx
    <iframe
      src={`https://dashboard.example.com/embed/form?reportId=${reportId}`}
      width="100%"
      height="400"
      frameBorder="0"
    />
    ```
  </CodeGroup>
</Expandable>

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Forms integrate with your CRM. Track leads from specific postcodes.
</Callout>
