## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Before you begin, ensure you have:

  - A valid email address for signup

  - Access to your agency's postcode or local area for hyper-local reports

  - Social media accounts for sharing (optional but recommended)
</Callout>

Market Trak offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Cancel anytime.

## Sign Up for Your Free Trial

Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link" title-type="p">
    Navigate to the Market Trak homepage at `https://marketrak.pro/pricing` and click **Start free 14 day trial**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete checkout" icon="user" title-type="p">
    Complete the checkout process, choose your password and create your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Market Trak. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="monitor" title-type="p">
    Log in at `https://marketrak.pro/auth`. You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate Your First Seller's Guide

Create a hyper-local report for your area in seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Report Type" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    From the dashboard, click **New Report** and choose **Seller's Guide**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Location" icon="map-pin" title-type="p">
    Input your postcode (e.g., `SW1A 1AA`) or select your patch on the map.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Add your agency logo and branding colors (default: `#3B82F6`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Click **Generate Report**. Download PDF or view live preview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your report includes live UK property data: average prices, sales trends, and buyer insights.
