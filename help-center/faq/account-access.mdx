## Overview

To access your account, sign in using your registered email.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://app.markettrak.com`
2. Click **Login**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **Sign In**

## Reset Password

If you've forgotten your password:

- Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
- Follow instructions to reset
