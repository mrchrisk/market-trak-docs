---
title: Can't Log In to Market Trak
description: How to quickly resolve login issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to sign in
- "Incorrect password" error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Ensure email and password are entered correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page and check your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    Try clearing cookies/cache and sign in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
