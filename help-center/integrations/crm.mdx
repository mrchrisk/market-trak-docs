Market Trak integrates with **Loop CRM** and **Street CRM**, the two most popular CRM systems for UK estate agents. Connecting your CRM unlocks powerful features like automatic property feed posts, in-chat CRM queries, and property data access.

## Supported CRMs

| CRM | Features |
|---|---|
| **Loop CRM** | Property search, buyer/seller data, automated property feed, AI assistant queries |
| **Street CRM** | Property search, buyer/seller data, automated property feed, AI assistant queries |

## Connecting your CRM

1. Go to **Integrations** (navigate to `/integrations`)
2. Find your CRM (Loop or Street)
3. Enter your **API key** (provided by your CRM provider)
4. Click **Connect**

Your API key is encrypted with AES-GCM encryption and stored securely. It is never exposed in the frontend.

### Where to find your API key

- **Loop CRM** - contact Loop support or check your Loop account settings for API access
- **Street CRM** - available in your Street CRM admin panel under API settings

## What you can do with a connected CRM

### Property Feed (automated posting)

The [Property Feed](/help-center/social-media/property-feed) monitors your CRM for new listings every 2 hours and automatically creates social media posts. This is the most popular reason to connect your CRM.

### AI Assistant CRM queries

With a CRM connected, the [AI Assistant](/help-center/ai-assistant/overview) can search your CRM data directly from chat:

- "Show me properties for sale in Chelmsford"
- "Find buyers with a budget over £500,000"
- "What properties have sold recently in CM1?"
- "Show me landlord contacts"

The assistant also gets CRM-specific [slash commands](/help-center/ai-assistant/slash-commands) like `/forsale`, `/buyers`, `/sold`, and more.

### Property posts from CRM data

When creating social media posts, you can pull property details from your CRM to create accurate, data-rich content about specific listings.

## Managing your CRM connection

### Checking status

The Integrations page shows:

- Which CRM is connected
- Whether the API key is valid
- Connection health

### Updating your API key

If your API key changes:

1. Go to Integrations
2. Click the edit button on your CRM connection
3. Enter the new API key
4. Save

The new key is encrypted and stored immediately.

### Disconnecting

To disconnect your CRM:

1. Go to Integrations
2. Click **Disconnect** on your CRM section
3. Confirm

After disconnecting:

- The Property Feed will stop detecting new listings
- CRM slash commands will disappear from the AI Assistant
- Existing posts created from CRM data are unaffected

## One CRM at a time

Each workspace connects to one CRM. If you switch from Loop to Street (or vice versa), disconnect the current CRM first, then connect the new one.

## Tips

- Keep your API key secure and do not share it
- If CRM queries return unexpected results, check that your API key has the correct permissions
- The Property Feed works best when your CRM data is up to date, make sure listings have accurate addresses and dates
