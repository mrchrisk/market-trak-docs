title: Custom Email Domains
description: Send email campaigns from your own domain using Mailgun integration.

By default, email campaigns are sent from a Market Trak email address. To send from your own domain (e.g. `updates@youragency.com`), you can connect a custom email domain via Mailgun.

## Why use a custom domain?

- **Professional appearance** - emails come from your agency domain, not a generic one
- **Better deliverability** - properly configured domains improve inbox placement
- **Brand consistency** - recipients see your brand in the "From" address
- **Trust** - clients are more likely to open emails from a domain they recognise

## Setting up a custom domain

### Prerequisites

- A domain you own (e.g. `youragency.com`)
- Access to your domain's DNS settings
- A Mailgun account (Market Trak handles the integration)

### Steps

1. Go to **Workspace Settings**
2. Navigate to the **Email** section
3. Click **Add Custom Domain**
4. Enter your domain name
5. Market Trak will provide you with DNS records to add:
   - **TXT records** for SPF and DKIM verification
   - **CNAME record** for Mailgun tracking
6. Add these records in your domain's DNS management panel
7. Wait for DNS propagation (usually a few minutes, sometimes up to 48 hours)
8. Click **Verify** in Market Trak to confirm the records are in place

Once verified, your campaigns will be sent from your custom domain.

## DNS records explained

| Record Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **SPF (TXT)** | Authorises Mailgun to send emails on behalf of your domain |
| **DKIM (TXT)** | Adds a cryptographic signature to prove emails are genuine |
| **Tracking (CNAME)** | Enables click and open tracking through your domain |

These records do not affect your existing email or website. They only authorise Mailgun to send on your behalf.

## Sending behaviour

After connecting a custom domain:

- **Campaign emails** - sent via Mailgun from your domain
- **Welcome emails** - sent via Mailgun from your domain
- **System emails** (password resets, verifications) - continue to be sent from Market Trak's system address

## Rate limits

Custom domains via Mailgun support higher sending rates:

- **Mailgun**: up to 1,000 emails per batch
- **Default (Resend)**: up to 100 emails per batch

This means campaigns go out faster with a custom domain.

## Troubleshooting

### Domain not verifying

- Double-check DNS records are entered exactly as shown (including the trailing period on some providers)
- Wait for DNS propagation, it can take up to 48 hours
- Ensure your domain registrar does not strip or modify TXT records

### Emails going to spam

- Verify your SPF and DKIM records are correctly configured
- Build sender reputation gradually, start with small campaigns
- Ensure your email content is not overly promotional or flagged by spam filters

## Tips

- Use a subdomain like `mail.youragency.com` or `updates.youragency.com` to separate marketing emails from your regular business email
- Check your domain verification status periodically in Workspace Settings
- A properly configured custom domain significantly improves email deliverability
