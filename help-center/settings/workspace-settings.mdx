Workspace settings control everything shared across your team: branding, email configuration, team members, and more.

## Accessing workspace settings

Click your **profile icon** in the sidebar, then select **Workspace settings**. Or navigate directly to `/workspace-settings`.

## Branding

Customise how your workspace looks across reports, emails, and public pages.

### Logos

Upload three logo variants:

- **Dark logo** - shown on light backgrounds (reports, emails)
- **Light logo** - shown on dark backgrounds (sidebar in dark mode)
- **Favicon** - small square icon for browser tabs and the collapsed sidebar

### Brand colour

Set your primary brand colour using a hex code or the colour picker. This colour is used on:

- Report call-to-action buttons
- Email buttons
- Accent elements throughout the app

### Cookie consent

Toggle whether your public report pages show a cookie consent banner for GDPR compliance.

## Team members

Manage who has access to your workspace.

### Inviting members

1. Enter the person's email address in the invitation field
2. Click **Send Invite**
3. They receive an email with a link to join

### Removing members

Click the remove button next to a team member's name to revoke their access.

### Roles

Team members share the same workspace data and usage allowances. All members have equal access to features within the workspace.

## Email settings

Configure your email sending preferences:

- **Sender name** - the name that appears in the "From" field of campaign emails
- **Reply-to address** - where lead replies are directed
- **Welcome email** - toggle automatic welcome emails for new leads

For custom sending domains, see [Email Domains](/help-center/integrations/email-domains).

## Lead capture settings

Configure how leads are captured from your public reports:

- **Lead gate** - require visitors to enter their details before viewing full report content
- **Fields** - choose which fields to collect (name, email, phone, etc.)
- **Webhooks** - forward captured leads to external systems via webhook URL
