User settings control your personal profile and preferences. These are specific to your account, not your workspace, so team members each have their own settings.

## Accessing user settings

Click your **profile icon** in the sidebar, then select **User settings**. Or navigate directly to `/user-settings`.

## Profile

Update your personal information:

- **Contact name** - your display name shown in the sidebar and to team members

- **Email address** - your login email (changing this requires verification)

- **Notification email** - optionally set a different email for notifications (if you want alerts sent somewhere other than your login email)

## Notification preferences

Control which notifications you receive and how:

| Event              | Email                                           | Push                      |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| **Report ready**   | When a report finishes generating               | Browser push notification |
| **New lead**       | When a new lead is captured                     | Browser push notification |
| **Campaign sent**  | When an email campaign completes                | Browser push notification |
| **Posts ready**    | When property feed posts are ready for approval | Browser push notification |
| **Credits low**    | When your credit balance is running low         | Browser push notification |
| **Post published** | When a scheduled post is published              | Browser push notification |

Toggle each notification on or off per channel. You can enable email notifications, push notifications, or both.

### Push notifications

To receive browser push notifications:

1. Click **Enable Push Notifications** in your notification settings

2. Your browser will ask for permission to show notifications

3. Once enabled, you will receive real-time alerts even when the Market Trak tab is not active

Push notifications work in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.

## AI Assistant settings

- **Custom instructions** - write permanent instructions for your AI assistant (see [Memory and Instructions](/help-center/ai-assistant/memory-and-instructions))

- **Memory toggle** - enable or disable the auto-learning memory feature
