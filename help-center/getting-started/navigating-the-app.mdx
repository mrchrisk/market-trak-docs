Market Trak has a clean, sidebar-driven layout that keeps every feature one click away.

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. It includes:

| Item             | Description                                                       |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Dashboard**    | Your command centre with metrics, awareness score, and quick wins |
| **Reports**      | Generate, view, edit, and share property market reports           |
| **Social Posts** | Create, schedule, and manage social media content                 |
| **Leads**        | Manage captured leads and run email campaigns                     |
| **AI Assistant** | Chat with your AI assistant for content creation and CRM queries  |

Some items may show a lock icon if they require a higher subscription tier.

### Collapsing the sidebar

Click **Collapse** at the top of the sidebar (next to the logo)to shrink it to icons only. Click the expand arrow to bring it back. Your preference is remembered.

## Profile menu

Click your **profile icon** at the bottom of the sidebar to open a popover with:

- **User settings** - personal profile and notification preferences

- **Workspace settings** - branding, team, and email configuration

- **Billing** - subscription management and credit purchases

- **Get help** - opens live chat support

- **Sign out**

## Mobile navigation

On mobile devices, the sidebar is replaced by:

- A **top header** with your logo and menu

- A **bottom navigation bar** with the main sections (Dashboard, Reports, Social, Leads)

The experience is fully responsive, so every feature works on your phone or tablet.

## Locked features

If a sidebar item shows a lock icon, it means your current plan does not include that feature. Click through to see upgrade options, or visit [Billing](/help-center/settings/billing) to change your plan.
