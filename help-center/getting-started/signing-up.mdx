title: Signing Up
description: Create your Market Trak account and start your free trial.
---

Getting started with Market Trak takes less than two minutes. You can sign up directly or through a Stripe checkout if you already know which plan you want.

## Creating your account

1. Go to [marketrak.pro](https://marketrak.pro) and click **Start Your Free Trial** or **Get Started**
2. Enter your email address and choose a password
3. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link
4. You will be redirected to your new dashboard

## Starting your free trial

Every new account begins with a free trial. During the trial you get full access to all features on your chosen plan so you can explore everything Market Trak offers before committing.

- Your trial starts automatically when you create your account
- You will receive reminder emails at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before your trial ends
- You can cancel at any time during the trial with no charge
- If you do not cancel, your subscription will begin automatically when the trial period ends

## Signing up via Stripe checkout

If you selected a plan from the [Pricing](https://marketrak.pro/pricing) page, you will be taken through Stripe checkout first. After completing payment details, your account is created and your trial begins.

## Accepting a team invitation

If someone has invited you to join their workspace:

1. Check your email for the invitation link
2. Click the link to open the **Accept Invite** page
3. If you already have an account, sign in. If not, create one
4. You will be added to the workspace automatically

## Signing in

Already have an account? Go to [marketrak.pro/auth](https://marketrak.pro/auth) and sign in with your email and password.

### Forgot your password?

On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?** to receive a password reset email. Follow the link to set a new password.

## Next steps

Once you are signed in, head to [Workspace Setup](/help-center/getting-started/workspace-setup) to configure your branding and team.
