Getting started with Market Trak takes less than two minutes. Signup happens through Stripe checkout so you can pick the plan that suits you from the start.

## Creating your account

1. Go to [marketrak.pro](https://marketrak.pro) and click **Start Your Free Week** or **Get Started**

2. Choose your plan on the pricing page

3. Enter your details in Stripe checkout and confirm

4. Set your password when prompted, then you will be redirected to your new dashboard

## Starting your free week

Every new account begins with a free week of the plan you picked during checkout. You get full access to all features on that plan so you can explore everything Market Trak offers before committing.

- Your free week starts automatically when you complete checkout

- You will receive a reminder email 1 day before your free week ends

- You can cancel at any time during the free week with no charge

- If you do not cancel, your subscription will begin automatically when the free week ends

## Signing in

Already have an account? Go to [marketrak.pro/auth](https://marketrak.pro/auth) and sign in with your email and password.

### Forgot your password?

On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?** to receive a password reset email. Follow the link to set a new password.

## Next steps

Once you are signed in, follow the [First Steps](/help-center/getting-started/first-steps) guide to get everything set up and ready to go.
