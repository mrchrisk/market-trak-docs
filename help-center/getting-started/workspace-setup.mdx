Your workspace is your company's home in Market Trak. Everything you create (reports, posts, leads, templates) belongs to your workspace. You can customise it with your branding, invite team members, and configure email settings.

## Accessing workspace settings

Click your **profile icon** in the sidebar, then select **Workspace settings**. You can also navigate directly to `/workspace-settings`.

## Branding

Make your reports, emails, and public pages look like they come from your agency.

### Logo

Upload your company logo in two variants:

- **Dark logo** - displayed on light backgrounds
- **Light logo** - displayed on dark backgrounds (e.g. the sidebar in dark mode)
- **Favicon** - a small square icon shown in browser tabs and the collapsed sidebar

### Brand colour

Set your primary brand colour. This is used on report buttons, email call-to-action buttons, and other accent elements. You can enter a hex code or use the colour picker.

### Cookie consent

Toggle whether a cookie consent banner appears on your public report pages. This helps you comply with GDPR and similar regulations.

## Team members

Invite colleagues to collaborate within your workspace.

1. In Workspace settings, find the **Team Members** section
2. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
3. Click **Send Invite**
4. They will receive an email with a link to join your workspace

Team members share the same workspace data (reports, leads, templates) and usage allowances.

## Email settings

Configure how emails are sent from your workspace:

- **Sender name** - the "From" name recipients see
- **Reply-to address** - where replies go
- **Welcome email** - toggle automatic welcome emails for new leads on or off

For custom email domains, see [Email Domains](/help-center/integrations/email-domains).
