title: Approval Queue

The Approval Queue is where automatically generated posts (from the [Property Feed](/help-center/social-media/property-feed)) land before going live. It gives you full control over what gets published on your social media accounts.

## Accessing the queue

1. Click **Social Posts** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Approval Queue** tab (or go to `/social-media/approval-queue`)

## Reviewing posts

Each post in the queue shows:

- The **property image** (AI-generated)
- The **caption** text
- The **source** (which CRM listing or feed it came from)
- The **target platform** (Facebook, Instagram)
- The **creation date**

## Actions

For each post in the queue, you can:

### Approve

Click **Approve** to move the post forward. Depending on your settings, it will either:

- Publish immediately to your connected Facebook page
- Be saved as a scheduled post for you to set a time

### Edit before approving

If the caption or image needs tweaking:

1. Click into the post to open the editor
2. Modify the caption text
3. Regenerate the image if needed
4. Then approve the updated version

### Reject

Click **Reject** to discard the post entirely. Rejected posts are removed from the queue and will not be published.

## Auto-approve

If you prefer hands-off automation, you can enable **auto-approve** for specific platforms in your Property Feed settings. When auto-approve is on, posts skip the queue entirely and publish (or schedule) automatically.

> **Caution:** Auto-approve means posts go live without your review. Make sure you are happy with the AI output quality before enabling this.

## Queue management tips

- Check the queue at least once a day when you have an active Property Feed
- Posts are most effective when published within a few days of the listing going live
- If your queue is building up, consider enabling auto-approve for one platform to reduce the backlog
- Edit captions to add your personal touch, a brief local insight makes a big difference
