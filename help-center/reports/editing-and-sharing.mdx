## Editing report content

When you view a report in your dashboard (the private view), you can click on text sections to edit them:

- **Market summary** - rewrite the AI-generated market overview in your own words

- **Market insight** - customise the insight box with your local expertise

- **Section text** - adjust any descriptive content throughout the report

Your edits are saved automatically. The public version of the report updates immediately.

> **Tip:** Editing is only available in your private (logged-in) view. Visitors to the public link see the report as read-only.

## Hiding and showing widgets

You can hide individual sections, stats, and data rows on any report type to create a cleaner, more focused report for your audience.

<Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-60a9d1e7-de11-4a54-9530-1c742f9a03b9/doc-db1aaa2f-b483-467d-95b5-4501879b6003/1774283428067-i3s1w3dwmdb-Hide-Widgets.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="997" height="606" alt="" />

### How it works

1. View the report in your private (logged-in) view

2. Hover over any section or stat to reveal an **eye icon** toggle

3. Click the eye to hide that widget

4. Hidden widgets appear as a collapsed placeholder in your private view so you can restore them later

5. In the public view, hidden widgets disappear completely and the layout reflows seamlessly

On mobile, the eye icons are always visible (no hover needed).

### What you can hide

The level of control depends on the report type:

- **Magazine Intelligence** - individual At a Glance stats, Price Breakdown rows, Market Activity cells, insight cards, timeline items, and recent sales entries

- **Data Sheet** - individual Property Price rows, Growth Analysis rows, Sales Activity cells, and Market Insight bullets

- **Sellers Guide** - entire sections, plus individual Market Trend, Market Demand, and Average Price cards

Visibility settings are saved per report and persist across sessions.

## Sharing reports

### Public link

Every report gets a unique public URL that anyone can view without signing in:

```
https://marketrak.pro/report/your-report-slug
```

To share a report:

1. Open the report from your Reports page

2. Click the **Share** button

3. Copy the public link

### Custom slugs

You can customise the URL slug for a more professional appearance. Instead of a random ID, use something memorable like:

```
https://marketrak.pro/report/brentwood-market-update-march-2026
```

Edit the slug in the report settings.

### QR codes

Each report can generate a QR code that links to its public page. This is ideal for:

- Printed flyers and brochures

- Window displays

- Business cards

- Physical marketing materials

Click the **QR Code** button on any report to download a high-resolution QR image.

## Report themes

Some reports styles support multiple visual themes:

- **Default** - clean, light layout

- **Dark** - professional dark background with light text

- **Emerald** - green-tinted theme

The theme affects the public report appearance.

## Lead capture

When someone views your public report, they can be prompted to enter their details before seeing the full content. This is the **lead gate**, and it automatically captures leads into your workspace. The visitor enters their details, the lead is captured immediately (the form no longer requires a 6-digit verification code), and the report unlocks on the same page. The webhook to your CRM fires the moment the form is submitted. \
\
See [Managing Leads](/help-center/leads/managing-leads) for more on how captured leads work.
