The dashboard is your command centre. It gives you an instant overview of your marketing activity, lead generation, and content performance across your workspace.

## Awareness Meter

The large featured tile at the top of your dashboard is the **Awareness Meter**. It scores your overall marketing activity on a scale from 0 to 100 based on:

- How many reports you have generated recently
- Email campaigns you have sent
- Social media posts you have created or scheduled
- Leads you have captured

The score decays over time if you are not active, encouraging consistent marketing habits. Think of it as your "marketing fitness" tracker.

## Metric cards

Below the Awareness Meter you will see quick-glance metric cards:

| Metric | What it tracks | Colour |
|---|---|---|
| **Leads** | New leads captured this period | Amber/Orange |
| **Campaigns** | Email campaigns sent | Green |
| **Social Posts** | Posts created and published | Purple |

Each card shows the current count plus a trend compared to the previous period. Hover over a card for additional detail.

## Today's Priority

The **Today's Priority** card suggests your next best action based on your recent activity. For example:

- "You haven't posted to social media this week" with a link to create a post
- "3 leads are waiting for a follow-up email" with a link to your leads page
- If everything is up to date, you will see an "All Caught Up" message

## Recent Activity

A timeline of your latest actions across the workspace:

- Reports generated
- Email campaigns sent
- Social media posts published
- Leads captured

Click any item to go directly to the relevant page.

## Refreshing data

Dashboard metrics update automatically and cache for 2 minutes to keep things fast. If you have just completed an action (like sending a campaign) and the numbers have not updated yet, wait a moment or click the refresh button.

Changes you make in one browser tab will sync to other open tabs automatically.
