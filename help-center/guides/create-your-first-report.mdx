## Overview

Create a branded report from your Market Trak dashboard in a few minutes.

<Callout kind="info">

This guide assumes you have a Market Trak account, can sign in to the dashboard, and have permission to create reports.

</Callout>

## Create your first report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Sign in to Market Trak and go to your main dashboard. Confirm you can see your recent activity and the **New Report** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start a new report">
    Click **New Report**. When prompted to choose a report type, select **Seller's Guide** or **Market Report**, depending on what you want to share.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the location">
    Add the area you want to cover by entering a postcode (for example, `SW1A 1AA`) or selecting your patch on the map. Check that the highlighted area matches your target market.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your branding">
    Upload your agency logo and set your branding color (default is `#3B82F6`). Preview the header to confirm your logo and color look correct on the first page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure report settings">
    Adjust any available filters, time range, or sections shown in the report so the content matches your audience. Use the on-screen preview to confirm the data and layout look right.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate and download">
    Click **Generate Report**. When the report finishes generating, either download it as **PDF** or **CSV**, or open the live preview to review it on screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you will get

Your first report includes local market data for the area you selected, wrapped in your agency branding so you can share it directly with clients.

Typical outputs:

- A **PDF** version suitable for emailing to clients or printing.
- A **CSV** export for working with the underlying data in spreadsheet tools.
- A **live preview** in your browser so you can quickly review and make adjustments before sharing.

## Troubleshooting

If something does not work as expected, check these common issues.

- **I cannot see the New Report button**  
  Confirm you are on the main dashboard, not within a specific settings or account page. If you still cannot see it, check with your account admin that you have report creation permissions.

- **My postcode or area is not accepted**  
  Make sure you enter a full, valid postcode with no extra spaces. If the map does not update, refresh the page and try selecting the area directly on the map instead of typing.

- **My logo looks blurry or cut off**  
  Upload a higher-resolution logo file and ensure it is not extremely wide or tall. After uploading, check the on-screen preview to confirm it fits in the header area.

- **Generate Report is disabled or does nothing**  
  Confirm you have selected a report type and entered a valid location. If the button is still disabled, check your internet connection and try again from a fresh browser tab.

- **The download does not start**  
  Allow pop-ups and downloads for Market Trak in your browser. If the file still does not download, try using a different browser or checking your downloads folder for a recently created file.
