## Overview

Follow these steps to create a market report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new report">
    Click **New Report** on your Market Trak dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select data sources">
    Choose the datasets you want to analyze (e.g., Sales, Trends).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure report settings">
    Select filters, time range, and desired output format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate & download">
    Click **Generate Report**; download the report as PDF or CSV.
  </Step>
</Steps>
