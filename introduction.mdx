## Overview

Market Trak empowers UK estate agents with hyper-local property market insights. You generate professional reports, create engaging social content, and capture high-quality leads to become the go-to agent in your area. Built on live UK property data, Market Trak turns market intelligence into your competitive edge.

Targeted exclusively at UK estate agents, the platform simplifies market analysis and content creation. You access Seller's Guides, Data Sheets, and Market Intelligence reports that position you as the local expert.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes. Follow these steps to create your first market report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your free 14-day trial account at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Area" icon="map-pin">
    Enter your postcode or select your local patch. Market Trak pulls live data automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Report" icon="file-text">
    Choose a report type and customize branding. Download or share instantly.

````javascript
// Example API call to generate report
const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/reports', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}` },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    postcode: 'SW1A 1AA',
    type: 'sellers-guide'
  })
});
````
